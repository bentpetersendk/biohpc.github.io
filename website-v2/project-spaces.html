PI to Project Space to Users

Project Spaces

A Project Space is a shared research environment owned by a PI. Users join Project Spaces rather than joining BioHPC as standalone accounts.

What a Project Space contains

Each Project Space brings the practical parts of collaborative research computing into one managed environment.

Storage

Shared areas for data, code, intermediate outputs, and research results.

Compute resources

Access to scheduled compute resources for approved members.

User membership

A defined list of users, collaborators, and Technical Contacts.

Resource accounting

Usage can be understood and reported at Project Space level.

Collaboration management

Membership follows PI approval and project needs.

Responsibility

The PI remains responsible for resource usage, access, and compliance.

PI and Technical Contact model

The PI remains the owner and responsible party for the Project Space. A PI may designate one or more Technical Contacts to assist with administration.

  • Technical Contacts may receive notifications.
  • They may assist with administration and request review.
  • They may approve requests on behalf of the PI when authorized.
  • The PI remains responsible for usage and compliance.

Need a Project Space?

Project Space requests should be submitted by the PI or by someone acting with PI approval.
